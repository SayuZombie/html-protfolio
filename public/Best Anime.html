The Best Anime by Sahil Keer

My top 5 anime all time

Dragon Ball

Our pint-sized hero, Goku, embarks on adventures under Master Roshi’s watchful eyes, battling intriguing foes, making friends (including the occasionally pervy Krillin and fiery Bulma), and seeking mystical Dragon Balls.

Naruto

Naruto (Part I): Spirited ninja Naruto Uzumaki trains, competes, and tangles with foes—plus, a fox demon spices things up.
Naruto: Shippuden (Part II): Teenage Naruto aims for Hokage glory, battles epic enemies, and Sasuke broods like a seasoned drama llama. 🍥🔥

One Piece

One Piece: Follow Monkey D. Luffy and his Straw Hat Pirate crew as they seek the legendary One Piece treasure across the Grand Line—a world of incredible abilities, colorful violence, political intrigue, and lore.

Hunter x Hunter

Gon and friends—Killua, Kurapika, and Leorio—navigate adventures, foes, and unexpected twists, from bright tournament arcs to harsh realities. 🌟🔍🐉

Attack on Titan

Eren Jaeger battles towering Titans, uncovers shocking truths, and seeks revenge in a world where walls hold secrets and humanity teeters on the edge. 🏴‍☠️🔍🐉
